Registered behavior
technician training.

Working as a Registered Behavior Technician can be exciting, fulfilling, and lucrative, and many employers consider it an important step to further roles in the behavioral healthcare field. However, it can be difficult to know how to get started or find an instructor that fits your needs. Behaviorly meets you where you are, offering both live interactive video training and self-paced training to prepare you for the RBT exam and the important work ahead.

Live Video Training

Live video training is a fully interactive RBT training experience with an experienced live instructor. Complete your 40 hour training through our live video classes on desktop or mobile, with available options every weekday. You may complete the course at your own pace as long as you finish within a year.

Our live training allows you to ask the instructor questions and interact with other prospective RBTs, clarifying points of instruction and drilling down on more difficult concepts. Live training includes examples and roleplay scenarios, as well as traditional instruction so you can get the most robust understanding possible of the terms, pitfalls, and best practices of behavioral healthcare.

Live video training is priced affordably, with most RBTs making up the tuition’s balance and cost of the exam within their first week of full-time work. This, plus the flexibility and ease of video training, make live video the preferred method of instruction for prospective RBTs learning with Behaviorly.

Self-Paced Video Training

Self-paced video training is the same course as our live training session, but able to be completed at any time of day. It can be the preferred solution for parents, working professionals, and students, and anyone else whose schedule limits their ability to attend a live class.

This style of training is more flexible, but you’ll be getting the same world-class instruction with our experienced instructors. With over 20,000 hours of cumulative teaching and over 2,000 students passed, we guarantee you’ll feel prepared for the exam and to start your career in behavioral healthcare.
