Welcome to a
new way to learn

Behaviorly is the simple way to gain remote certification as a Registered Behavior Technician. With live or self-paced 40 hour training courses, 1-on-1 mentorship, and individualized exam prep, our courses help bridge the gap between terminology and hands-on experience to ensure a new level of job readiness and confidence for RBTs. Welcome to your new career. Welcome to Behaviorly.

Three ways to RBT Success

You want the best of both worlds when training for RBT certification. Live or self-paced video training and individual mentorship prepare you with the RBT skills and techniques for the important behavioral healthcare work ahead.
Live Video Training

Live video training allows you to ask the instructor questions and interact with other prospective RBTs, clarifying points of instruction and drilling down on more difficult concepts. Live training includes examples and roleplay scenarios, as well as traditional instruction so you can get the most robust understanding possible of the terms, pitfalls, and best practices of behavioral healthcare.

Learn More
Self-Paced Video Training

We heard from parents, professionals, and students that they wanted a more flexible learning timeline. Self-paced training can be completed at your own pace, on your own time, with the same world-class instructors as the live training.
For any concepts that aren’t completely clear, you may schedule 1-on-1 mentoring with an instructor to ensure you feel as prepared as possible before your assessment.

Learn More
Live Mentoring

If you need additional instruction on any subject of the RBT certification assessment, schedule a live mentorship session with one of our experienced instructors. Our instructors will walk you through the concepts, providing real-world examples and roleplaying scenarios that speak to the challenges you may face in the assessments and the workplace.
