Once you’re ready for the final step.

Behaviorly offers easy assessment and
a path to your certification exam.

The Behavior Analyst Certification Board (BACB) is responsible for certifying Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) and Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs). The BACB requires successful completion of a 40-hr training course and a skills competency assessment in order to apply for the exam. Behaviorly offers training and virtual skills competency assessments so you can get your instruction and complete your assessment all in one place.
