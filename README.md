# Behaviorly

Welcome to the central repository for **Behaviorly**, a comprehensive platform dedicated to providing remote, high-quality certification and training for Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs). Behaviorly aims to bridge the gap between terminology and hands-on experience by offering accessible 40-hour training courses (both live and self-paced), 1-on-1 mentorship, individualized exam preparation, and skills assessments.

## Project Structure. 

This project directory serves as the centralized workspace for all of Behaviorly's business, marketing, product, and technical assets. It is organized into the following key domains:

### 🌐 Site (`/Site`)
The static frontend website for Behaviorly, showcasing our offerings and allowing users to sign up or request consultations.
- Built with HTML5, Bootstrap 5, and custom CSS.
- Includes key landing pages: `index.html`, `certification.html`, `individuals.html`, `teams.html`, `products.html`, and `resources.html`.
- Features a PHP-based webform for contact requests.

### 🎨 UX & User Stories (`/UX & User Stories`)
The product design and functional requirements driving the Behaviorly platform.
- **Customer Journey Maps & User Flows:** Visualizing the path users take from discovery to certification.
- **User Stories & Functionality:** Detailed requirements for the LMS, including progress tracking, module completion, and assessment scoring.
- **Site Structure & CTA Documents:** Outlines the architecture of the website and key call-to-action strategies.

### 📈 Marketing (`/Marketing`)
Strategic assets and planning documents for brand growth and outreach.
- **Social Media Strategy & Content Calendar:** Extensive competitor analysis, strategy audits, and planned content for the year.
- **Playbooks & Notes:** The core marketing playbook and ongoing strategic notes.
- **Interviews:** RBT interview questions and screening documents to better understand our target audience.

### 💼 Sales (`/Sales`)
Outreach and conversion materials for the sales team.
- **Contact & Hit-Lists:** Trackers for potential leads and target organizations.
- **Templates:** Standardized sales emails, talking points, and outreach templates.
- **Collateral:** Promotional flyers (e.g., `Behaviorly Flyer.pdf`) used for B2B and B2C sales.

### 📚 LMS Research (`/LMS Research`)
Documentation and analysis for selecting the optimal Learning Management System to host our 40-hour training courses.
- Includes detailed breakdowns and feature comparisons of various LMS providers to ensure our technical platform meets our user story requirements (such as time tracking and score reporting).

## Core Offerings
- **Live Video Training:** Interactive sessions with instructors and peers, including roleplay scenarios.
- **Self-Paced Training:** Flexible learning timelines for parents, professionals, and students.
- **1-on-1 Mentoring:** Live mentorship sessions for difficult concepts and exam preparation.
- **Assessments & Test Prep:** Compliant skills assessments and comprehensive study materials (quizlets, practice exams).

## Getting Started (Website)
To view the site locally:
1. Navigate to the `/Site` directory.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. *Note: The contact form (`webform.php`) requires a local PHP server environment to process form submissions.*
